\Hnote{O sweet Mother of the Redeemer,}
\Hnote{who abidest the open door of heaven, and star of the sea,}
\Hnote{Give aid to a falling people who strives to rise;}
\Hnote{O Thou who, nature wondering, begot thy holy Father,}
\Hnote{Virgin before and after, receiving that `Ave' from the mouth of Gabriel,}
\Hnote{have mercy on sinners.}
