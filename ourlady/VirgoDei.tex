\flagverse{2} Vera fides Géniti\\*
Purgávit crímina mundi\\
Et tibi virgínitas\\*
Invioláta manet.

\flagverse{3} Te matrem pietátis,\\*
Opem te clámitat orbis:\\
Subvénias famulis,\\*
O benedícta tuis.

\flagverse{4} Glória magna Patri\\*
Compar sit glória Nato,\\
Spirítui Sancto\\*
Glória magna Deo.

\Hlnote{O Virgin Mother of God, He whom the world could not contain\\
enclosed Himself in thy womb and was made man.}

\Hlnote{True faith in thy Son has cast out the sins of the world,\\
and thy virginity remains inviolate.}

\Hlnote{Thou art the Mother of divine love,
Thou the aiding power the world cries out to:\\
come in aid, O blessed one,
to thy servants.}

\Hlnote{Great glory be to the Father, equal glory to the Son,
great glory to God the Holy Spirit.}
